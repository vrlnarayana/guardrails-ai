A — Tabbed single page

Two top-level tabs (Prompt / Agent), sub-tabs per demo. Everything lives on one URL. Simplest to build.

Pros

  • Fast to build
  • Easy to navigate

Cons

  • Gets crowded with 9 demos
  • No individual URLs per demo

C — Guided tour / wizard

Step-by-step walkthrough that builds concepts progressively. Best for pure teaching, weakest for free exploration.

Pros

  • Best for beginners
  • Tells a story

Cons

  • Frustrating for devs who want to jump around
  • Complex state management
